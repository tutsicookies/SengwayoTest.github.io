Estate Late Matter and Wills

When an individual passes away, their assets, debts, and other financial matters need to be settled, and this process is often overseen by an executor or administrator. The administration of an estate usually involves tasks such as locating and valuing assets, paying off debts and taxes, and distributing the remaining assets to the beneficiaries named in the will.

Civil Dispute

Is a legal disagreement between two or more individuals or organizations that typically involves private matters, such as breach of contract, defamation, property disputes, or personal injury claims . .Civil disputes are typically resolved through a court proceeding, arbitration, or other alternative dispute resolution methods Seeking legal advice from an attorney who specializes in civil litigation can help individuals understand their rights and achieve the desired outcome.

Road Accident Fund Claims

The RAF is a government entity that provides compensation to victims of car accidents who can prove that their injuries were caused by the negligent or reckless actions of another party. The claim process can be complex, involving legal proceedings, negotiations, and potentially court appearances, and it is often recommended to seek the assistance of a lawyer specializing in RAF claims to navigate through the process effectively.

Criminal Law

Criminal law cases involve legal proceedings that arise when someone is accused of committing a crime that is considered an offense against society, such as theft, assault, murder, or drug trafficking. These cases typically involve a prosecutor representing the government, seeking punishment for the defendant, and a defence attorney representing the accused individual. The criminal justice system aims to ensure that individuals accused of crimes receive a fair trial and, if found guilty, are appropriately punished according to the law.

Conveyancing and Bond Registration

Conveyancing is the legal process of transferring property ownership from one person to another .It involves the preparation of legal documentation, compliance with legal requirements, and the registration of the property transfer in the Deeds Office [2][5]. Bond registration, on the other hand, is the process of registering a mortgage bond over immovable property as security for a loan or other obligations by a financial institution .The bond is registered in the Deeds Office and provides the financial institution with the right to sell the property if the borrower defaults on the loan

Medical Negligence

Medical negligence can include misdiagnosis, medication errors, surgical mistakes, birth injuries, and other instances where a healthcare professional's actions or omissions cause harm .In order to prove medical negligence, it is important to establish that the healthcare provider acted below the accepted standard of care and that this failure directly caused the patient's injuries or harm. If you believe you have been a victim of medical negligence, consulting with a medical malpractice attorney can help you understand your legal rights and options for seeking compensation.

Land Claims

Land claims refer to the process of pursuing legal rights to ownership, use, or control of land by individuals or groups, often based on historical, cultural, or legal precedents. These claims can arise due to various reasons, such as disputed territorial boundaries, historical injustices, indigenous land rights, or government policies that affect land use and ownership. Land claims can be a complicated legal process that may involve multiple parties, negotiations, and legal proceedings.
